Design Process

Our Way of Work

Fill The Brief

Share Your Needs & Aim

Kindly describe your requirements and design inspirations to help us understand your vision. Should you require our expert design advice, let us know, and we can schedule a call..

Check Your Email

Ready for the Pricing

Review the email for a detailed invoice with deliverables and pricing. It'll aid your decision to proceed with our services. We're available for a call at your convenience.

First Payment

Pay the First Installment

After reaching an agreement, we will send an invoice to you for the initial half. Payment options include PayPal and debit/credit cards. Please note, the payment is non-refundable.

First Draft

Get Ready for the First Glimpse

You'll receive the initial design draft, whether a sketch or digital proposal depending on the items. Once you approve it, we'll proceed in the same direction.

Second Payment

Pay The Remaining Installment

Upon design approval, we'll invoice the second half payment. Use available payment methods for settlement. After approval, receive final high-resolution files.

Final File Delivery

And Finally, Use the Branding

After payment, we'll send your final files via a link or folder with all your ordered items. Rest assured, the files will be sized appropriately for your streaming platform's needsa.
